Are you interested in Japanese culture? Whether you are looking to have a good time or just want to explore the many exciting tourist attractions that Japan has to offer, the Japanese Tour Society is for you. Spend your week at Tokyo and Kyoto... all for FREE! We are offering to fund your trip to Japan. Have a chance to visit all these amazing places!

Have Fun

Our mission is to provide individuals the opportunity to visit and experience the culture in Japan. Those who are unable to go to Japan due financial difficulties and have been wanting to go are then able to. Life is short and we believe that money should not be the barrier to having fun.

OUR TEAM, MORE THAN 100 STRONG, WORK HARD TO CONNECT PEOPLE WITH TRAVELING OPPORTUNITIES TO EXPERIENCE PLACES AROUND THE WORLD AND OUR MISSION IS TO MAKE PEOPLE HAPPY.
