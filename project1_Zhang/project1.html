What is Climate Change

Most people by now have heard of the term climate change but might not necessarily know the real meaning of it. Climate Change, as the name suggests has to do with the changes in humidity and temperatures of a particular area. Due to human actions, these once-natural factors are now posing a threat to earth's natural ecosystems. Animals and the landscapes that surround them are negatively impacted by climate change. As such, global warming is one causation of this man-made phenomenom.
